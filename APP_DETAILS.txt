APP DETAILS

App Name: ThinkStack

Short Description:
Boost learning with smart notes, mind maps, and spaced repetition flashcards.

Long Description:
Master your studies and organize your thoughts with ThinkStack, the ultimate offline companion for efficient learning. ThinkStack combines powerful note-taking with scientific study methods to help you remember more in less time.

Key Features:
*   Smart Note-Taking: Create and organize detailed notes into notebooks.
*   Visual Mind Maps: Visualize connections between ideas with an integrated mind-mapping tool.
*   Spaced Repetition System (SRS): Built-in flashcards use active recall algorithms to ensure you review concepts at the perfect time.
*   Study Timer: Stay focused and manage your study sessions effectively.
*   Progress Tracking: Visualize your learning progress and review statistics to stay motivated.
*   100% Offline: Access all your data anytime, anywhere, without an internet connection.
*   Dark & Light Modes: A beautiful, responsive interface that adapts to your preference.
